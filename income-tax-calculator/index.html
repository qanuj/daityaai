How to use this Income Tax Calculator

Enter your Gross Total Income (salary + other sources). Add eligible deductions under sections like 80C (PPF, ELSS, life insurance up to ₹1.5L). Select your age group for any senior citizen benefits. The calculator uses the latest New Tax Regime slabs for FY 2025-26 and automatically applies the 4% Health & Education Cess.

New Tax Regime Slabs (FY 2025-26)

  • ₹0 – ₹3 Lakh: 0%
  • ₹3 – ₹7 Lakh: 5%
  • ₹7 – ₹10 Lakh: 10%
  • ₹10 – ₹12 Lakh: 15%
  • ₹12 – ₹15 Lakh: 20%
  • Above ₹15 Lakh: 30%

Standard deduction of ₹50,000 is applied. This tool helps you quickly estimate your tax liability and take-home salary.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which tax regime should I choose?

The New Regime is now the default. It offers lower tax rates but fewer deductions. Use this calculator to compare your actual liability.

Is standard deduction included?

Yes, ₹50,000 standard deduction is automatically factored in for salaried individuals.

Are the slabs updated for 2025?

Yes, this calculator uses the New Tax Regime slabs applicable for Assessment Year 2026-27 (FY 2025-26).
